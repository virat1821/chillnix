About CHILLNIX

CHILLNIX Cooling Solutions is a trading name of Iseland Cooling Services Ltd. With more than 50 years of combined experience, we specialise in the supply, installation and maintenance of high-performance refrigeration cabinets, cold rooms and climate-controlled storage systems across Scotland and the UK.

We proudly support a wide range of businesses including:

  • Takeaways
  • Independent retailers
  • Convenience stores & supermarkets
  • Hospitality & food service providers
  • Pharmaceutical & industrial clients

Whether you need a single refrigeration unit or a complete cold-room fit-out, our team delivers efficient, durable and energy-smart solutions.
